What is Flow?

Flow is a freestyle rap app for DJs and Rappers. On the app, DJs can upload their beats for rappers to discover and freestyle to. When freestyling, rappers can shuffle through beats, record themselves, and edit their recordings. If they like a beat, they can reach out directly to the DJ to set up a formal recording. Lastly, beats are rated after every freestyle session to help promote the most fire tracks. Altogether, Flow lifts up the art form of freestyling by connecting rappers and DJs.
