NCAA Football 2023

College football is coming back.

Personal Project | 2022 | 2 months


What is the NCAA Football series?

The NCAA Football series is one of the most popular sports video games in history. The series has thrived since 1993, capturing the college football gameday experience with legendary game modes and customization. In 2014, the series was forced to be discontinued for not compensating college football players. Come 2022, however, the NCAA has implemented more fair rules for players to profit off of their name, image, and likeness. Thus, the series looks to make a legendary return by 2024.

Project Overview

NCAA Football means the world to me, and it means the universe to my friends. After hearing about NIL and the return of the NCAA FB series, I felt eager to investigate the world of opportunities out there. After all, this is just the start of a historic uproar making magic from college sports. Within this world, I took the hypothetical stance of a UXR Consultant for EA Sports.

To kick off this project, I picked the brains of 4 die-hard CFB fans with pizza, playtesting, and even crazy 8s. From these sessions, I recommended to delay the game and implement more features, and coincidentally, EA followed suit (in real life). With this fate, I continued research into the Fall of 2022, using a MaxDiff survey to prioritize new feature ideas. In the end, I couldn't be happier with this story, and the twists and turns it takes with such a passionate fanbase at the wheel.

Problem

Challenge

EA Sports produces two football game series: NCAA Football and Madden NFL. Over the years, the two have felt starkly different; while NCAA is built with creativity and spirit, Madden is fueled by modern game tech. With NCAA being discontinued since 2014, a question arises of how to best iterate on its past games without feeling like up-to-date Madden games.

With such a highly anticipated return, NCAA Football 2023 cannot disappoint; it needs to live up to the hype. To save disappointment, die hard fans are passionate about the game being developed completely separate from Madden NFL. To live up to the hype, NCAA FB 2023 should emulate all that‚Äôs changed in college football over the years. Accounting for all this, we must ideate with the fans themselves; this way, we can truly grasp the question: What must we keep? change? update?
