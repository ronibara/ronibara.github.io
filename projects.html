Things I've Done in Skool

Creative AI: Love Haikus

My group implemented language models that trained on Beatles lyrics to generate their own lyrics to a song. Then, we tweaked the functions and coded some more heuristics to generate haikus from the same n-gram training technique. We replaced Beatles lyrics with lyrics from love songs on my "sad" playlist. Through a twitterbot, the program tweets a haiku every time it runs.

Downtown Boxing Gym PSA

I interviewed Coach Khali Sweeney, a CNN hero and founder of the Downtown Boxing Gym. His gym is a nonprofit for kids that prioritizes books (school) before boxing. Khali also serves as a role model for these kids to look up to as they face the struggles of urban inequality. To visualize Sweeney's purpose, I created a stop-motion PSA on youtube.
