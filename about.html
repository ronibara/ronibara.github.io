Hey I'm Rohan, I'm a Sophomore, Indian American from Northville (MI), ex-hockey player, and aspiring friend to all. I'm pursuing the UX track at UofM's School of Information. I first heard of UX from design-related passions like making class memes, whipping up aesthetic powerpoints, and just graphic design altogether; however, as I've come to practice and learn more, I now I love UX for its research side; particularly, for equipping me with the scientific methods to break down a problem, all while still taking an artistic approach.

Personally, I like to make the people around me feel happy and comfortable, which stems from little things like my love for standup comedy and my hate for crumbs. Standup fascinates me because each comedian has their own artistic way of getting a laugh out of an audience. Likewise, I hope to bring this energy to the groups of people I get to lead or work with. On campus, I've been a Co Director at TEDxUofM, where I had the privilege to practice leading others in an environment fostering acceptance and curiosity to spread ideas worth sharing.

Altogether, I enjoy the problem space of any situation on campus, from classes to clubs to side projects, as I can add method to the madness one sticky note at a time. Outside of school, I'm excited to look for user research opportunities that work on problems I'm passionate for; thanks so much for taking the time today to meet with me!
